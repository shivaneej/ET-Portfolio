Quizziz

Quizziz allows assessing student knowledge with confidence levels and identify misconceptions effortlessly. It offers adaptive questions and reflection-based answers for personalized learning.

Project Overview

Quizizz is a learning platform that offers multiple tools to make a classroom fun, engaging, and interactive! Teachers can create gamified Quizzes and Lessons, conduct formative assessments, host live activities or assign them as homework, tap into detailed performance reports, and so much more! This section of the portfolio showcases the improvements that our team made to the Quizziz desktop app to assess prior knowledge.

The Problem

  • No feature to assess students' prior knowledge
  • No open-ended questions
  • No way to assess the difficulty of the quiz
  • No feedback and explanation for incorrect answers
  • No way to differentiate between guessing and actually knowing

Prior Knowledge

Prior knowledge refers to the information, understanding, and skills a learner already has before encountering new content or experiences. It forms the basis for acquiring new knowledge and plays a crucial role in how individuals learn and integrate new information. Skilled educators assess and utilize their students' existing knowledge to enhance learning, adapting their teaching methods to fill gaps and build on what students already know. Recognizing and incorporating existing knowledge can promote deeper learning and improve retention of new concepts, which is a key focus of our work on Quizziz.

Features

Pre Quiz Surveys

Don't assume that the students will use their prior knowledge before taking a Quiz! Present a survey to trigger recall before every quiz.
Pre-Quiz is an excellent way to identify which areas require more improvements by providing a comprehensive results of your strengths and weaknesses.

Confidence Assessment

Knowing and Understanding are not the same. Use slider with discrete steps instead of slider with continuous values to minimize thinking efforts.
The Confidence Assessment measures how confident students are in their answers. Quizziz provides a detailed Confidence Score which is calculated by considering both the correctness of the answers and the confidence level that was assigned to each answer. Answers with higher confidence levels are given more weight in the calculation, while answers with lower confidence levels have less impact on the score. This means that being confident about correct answers boosts the score, while being overconfident about incorrect answers may lower it. The score helps pinpoint areas for improvement. By monitoring the confidence levels and their correlation with correctness, learners can enhance their learning process.

Elaborative Prompts

The inclusion of open-ended questions will trigger the students to actively engage in the thinking process and apply their prior knowledge to the specific context.
The answer will be graded by AI, and users can see the grading rubric, and some of the top scoring answers. Users can also request for manual grading in case they are not satisfied with the AI grading.

Misconception Identification

If users end up selecting an incorrect answer, Quizziz provides them the ability to select their reasoning behind the chosen answer so that the misconception can be identified and cleared. Based on the analysis of misconception, Quizziz will provide links to recommended readings.

Adaptive Difficulty

Student performance is enhanced when prior knowledge is activated, so easier questions cover foundational material and prompt connections to harder questions. Students should be presented with learning at the right levels. If the student struggles on easy questions, the quiz ends early and suggests easier quizzes.
